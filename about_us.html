Our Institutions

COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

ABES Institute of Technology (AKTU College Code 290) embarked on its mission in the field of Technical Education in the year 2007. With NBA accreditation of majority of its B. Tech. programs and alumni spread globally; it is a name to reckon with in Engineering Education. Facilitated with Centres of Excellence in the domains of Big-Data – Datreon, IoT – Entuple & CDAC, Analog & Embedded System – Texas Instruments, Power Electronics & Drives – Entuple & CDAC, Industrial Automation Pneumatic Systems – Festo and many more, the students are responsive to real-world problems which is evident from their recognitions at National and International level apart from enabling them to become job fit and industry ready.

COLLEGE OF PHARMACY

ABESIT College of Pharmacy (AKTU College Code 913) has inherited legacy of, ABESIT Group, a brand name in Engineering Education. With right expertise, technology, facilities and staff, we offer B.Pharm. program in Pharmacy with the aim to develop qualified healthcare professionals with a high degree of integrity and ethical standards. We at ABESIT College of Pharmacy, ensure that our students are well adept at the futuristic skills required to remain employable and job fit.
